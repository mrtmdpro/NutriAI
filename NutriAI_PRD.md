# **Product Requirements Document (PRD): NutriAI** 

# **1\. Executive Summary**

NutriAI is an evidence-based, interactive nutritional intelligence platform designed where you can find visualized, easy-to-digest scientific information to make informed decisions about your nutritional supplements or your decisions on finding one. We empower consumers to make safe, informed nutritional decisions while establishing a definitive standard for transparency in the wellness market. 

In the future, by leveraging computer vision and heavily grounded AI, NutriAI transforms dense, scientifically complex supplement data into highly digestible, rigorously vetted insights. 

**Brand Positioning & Monetization Strategy**

* **B2C Free Tier (The Showcase):** Access to the core search engine, foundational supplement database, KOL educational feeds, and limited monthly label scans.  
* **B2C Premium Tier (NutriAI Pro):** Unlimited AI label scanning, personalized daily adherence tracking, advanced analytics dashboards, and deep-dive scientific literature summaries.  
* **B2B Enterprise (Data & Integration):** API access for telehealth providers to cross-reference patient supplements with medication contraindications, and anonymized consumer trend data for clinical researchers and ethical supplement manufacturers.

**2\. Business Requirements (BR)**

**BR 1: The Evidence-Based Knowledge Hub (top priority)**

* **Objective:** Establish absolute consumer trust through a rigorously curated, fully traceable nutritional database.  
* **Core Features:**  
  * **Scientific Search Engine:** A high-precision search bar returning verifiable data on compounds, efficacies, and clinical trials with **ranking in quality and pricing of that supplement in the market (money-making) in each posts**   
  * **Independent Quality Index Page:** A transparent, tiered ranking system evaluating market supplements based purely on third-party lab testing, ingredient quality, and price-per-dose.  
  * **Curated Content Feed:** Visual, scientifically backed editorial content and verified KOL (Quý Somsen,...) video integrations, strictly filtered for medical accuracy  **Personalized Feed of your own supplement products:** Revolving around the latest evidence or any informational video regarding the usage of your products (also with **ranking in quality and pricing of that supplement in the market (money-making) in each.**

**BR 2: Adherence Tracking & Analytics Dashboard**

* **Objective:** Drive daily active usage (DAU) and improve user health outcomes through behavioral reinforcement.  
* **Core Features:**  
  * **Smart Logging:** A frictionless interface to log daily supplement intake.  
  * **Notification Engine:** Customizable, timed alerts to ensure consistent adherence to nutritional protocols.  
  * **Performance Analytics:** A personalized dashboard tracking long-term adherence trends, allowing users to visually correlate their supplement routine with their self-reported wellness goals.

**BR 3: Computer Vision Label Analysis (NutriScan)**

* **Objective:** Eliminate consumer confusion at the point of purchase or consumption.  
* **Core Features:**  
  * **Optical Character Recognition (OCR):** Users capture a photo of any supplement label.  
  * **Nutritional Translation Engine:** The system extracts the proprietary blend data and cross-references it with the NutriAI database to explain exactly what each ingredient does in plain language.  
  * **Safety & Dosage Check:** Flags potential redundancies (e.g., taking two different supplements that both contain high doses of Vitamin D) and suggests optimized alternatives.

**BR 4: Grounded Conversational AI Assistant**

* **Objective:** Provide instant, personalized, and context-aware nutritional education.  
* **Core Features:**  
  * **RAG-Powered Architecture:** An AI assistant restricted to pulling answers exclusively from NutriAI’s proprietary, clinically vetted database to ensure high fidelity and safety.  
  * **Contextual Linking:** Every claim made by the AI generates a direct citation link back to the source literature or internal NutriAI article.
